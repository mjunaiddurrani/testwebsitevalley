Animated motion graphics video production requires a fine budget and set of skills that are competitive enough to handle all the technical aspects. A complete team works together to generate a great video but still, sometimes the response of video is not appropriate. Maybe because producers and brand owners neglect some aspects that are essential for the creation of a great piece of art. Script

It is commonly assumed that for an animated motion graphics video only technical skills are enough. But in actual creativity, concept and a script all have their own value. Script directly supports the animation and in case there are some loopholes in your animation, only a good script can cover them well.  Script provoke the audience to view the video till its end.

Technology

Advancement of technology is essential to give a new life to animation. Speed, colors, lights and overall look all suffer a lot when production team uses outdated technology. For 3D animation the more you invest in technology the more productive results you receive.

Tools

There are a number of platforms where you can create animated motion graphics. Each of the programs allows users to avail certain tools. According to your niche, you can also install add-ons for ease and quality of work. Some tools are free whereas for many you have to pay some bucks. Smart use of tools can help production team to create attractive animations in less time.

Realistic

Try to make every element, character, and background look realistic. The more real it looks the more audience get attracted to it. No matter you are creating a cartoon or fictional character or there are only products in the video, still, the things should look close to real. For example along with characters also focus on shadows.

Application

PowToon, Sparkol, Wideo, Moovly, WhatFix are some of the applications that can help production team to create amazing animated motion graphics videos. Many applications provide free templates so that you can start animation process in an organized way.

]]> To become a better web designer is a never-ending struggle as there is no limit to the creative ways through which you can enhance the layout of the website. To create an online masterpiece, a designer has to explore the depth of technical possibilities, experiment unique ideas and take sheer risks.

The layout of the website reflects the designer’s talent and efforts but hard work is not all you need to bring out the best results. For designing an attractive website, the designers should take notice of some tips and tricks through which they can amplify their efficiency. You need to go through these ingenious ideas to hone your web designing skills.

  1. Pay Attention to Latest Techniques

Web designing industry is heavily driven by trends. You do not necessarily need to follow trends but to get to the top of all you need to at least pay attention to the latest designing techniques being used commercially. Be a competent critique, develop an eye for holding the best ideas and master the skills of executing them in the most unique and inspiring ways.

  1. Avoid Thinking of Coding While Layout Designing

Our will to experiment unique techniques often wavers when we start thinking about coding even before planning the layout. Your first draft of design should be relentlessly creative and based entirely on imagination without technical concerns. Develop the mock-up in Photoshop first and then dig into coding it.

  1. Banner Slideshow Won’t Work

The trend of slideshow/image carousel previously became very popular but it doesn’t seem to outshine anymore. This layout is although inclusive in terms of information but with escalating competition in the web market, the user hardly stays that long to go through all banners. Be more particular and well-defined. Decide one prominent feature of your product and present it in the most appealing style.

  1. Use Simpler Navigation

We have come a long way beyond all those traditional headers or sidebars flooded with links and tabs. Simplify the navigation by reducing the links. Avoid using dropdown and multi-tier menus as they often overwhelm the users.

  1. Slide Away Social Media Icons

The important information about your product should take the most prominent place on your webpage, not the social media icons. Placing these icons on header would be an unwise thing to do as you will invite users to leave just before they fully arrive. Place the icons in the footer and let users connect with your product first and then move further to find out more.

  1. The Lesser the Better

If you want to design elegant and sophisticated website, master the skill of using the white-space efficiently. Just hone this one hack and you can do wonders with your website designs. Observe and pay attention to minimal websites and learn to give your website enough room to breathe.

  1. Make Font Size Bigger and Colors Bolder

In web design, typography and colors are incredibly important as they draw most of the attention of the user. Make your font stand out and clearly visible by increasing its size. Take inspiration from the beauty of nature and extract vibrant and contrasting palette of colors for your web landscape.

Now you know the drill and technique to play with your creativity. Just go ahead and create wonders.
