]]> In a crowded market, it is necessary to increase sales, profits, and successful businesses. The basic objective of marketing is to create a brand of products and services. Let’s take a closer look at why brand identity is important to your business.

What Is Brand Identity?

A brand identity is a set of tools that a company uses to create the image of a brand. Brand image is the perception of customers about a brand, which consists of several associations related to the brand image and the interaction of memory with the brand.

The brand image and its elements are due to the company’s mission, the brand’s value proposition, long-term objectives, competitiveness in the market, and the connection between the significance and the interest of the target audience. These elements have essential characteristics, and the branding process explains what the company wants to convey. On the other hand, brand identity shows how these basic elements are transmitted. The most commonly recognized aspects of brand identity are generally:

  • Brand name.
  • Tagline or slogans.
  • Color and graphic style.
  • Logos and word-marks and their distinctions.
  • Voice and intonation.
  • Typography and styles.

These basics can be grouped in different ways, and there are many opinions about the elements of the brand to be included in this list and their order. These transformations are usually clarified by discussing the brand’s history and the opinions of specific experts.

What better example than the Coca Cola brand? They put the brand at the forefront of their marketing strategy, and the results are well known. At first glance, few people associate or recognize this brand. Now let’s look at some of the main reasons how brand identity can affect your business.

Branding Helps Recognize Your Products Or Services

The brand is more than just a tag, logo design, and a collection of stylish slogans. Anything that helps to embrace an organization and differentiate it from other users. For example, a brand can be implemented as follows:

  • The visual identity of the brand (website, logo, color, etc.).
  • Marketing and communication.
  • Product and packaging.
  • In-store familiarity.
  • Partnership with sponsors.

Think of the brand as human. Each has its personality, dress, communication style, values, friends, qualities, and stories. They are what make us, and these qualities constitute the brand.

It Brings More Business

Brand identity is important when you want to expand your existing businesses and create new ones. Well-known brands are easy to identify and can bring more business to a company and thus increase visibility or popularity in a niche market. This makes customers a more attractive option and a unique position to get your business to market.

It Supports Workforce Satisfaction

When employees run a high-end company, they are happy and proud of their work. Working with a reputable brand brings high ratings to your employees and makes your business more enjoyable and satisfying. A branded workplace allows employees to feel more satisfied and connected to the organization. Employees are proud to use promotional products on desktop computers.

It Helps Gain Stakeholders’ Trust

Creating a brand strategy is not just about increasing the number of customers in an organization or attracting the best talent. Branding can help a company build its reputation not only to attract customers and employees but also to attract investors, business partners, service providers, governments, opinion leaders, buyers, licensees, etc. Service providers want to collaborate with well-known companies, for example, and because of their experience.

It Benefits Marketing

Marketing or advertising is another part of brand building, and the advertising strategy your company uses affects brand awareness and how you want it to appear. Billboard is the most common form of advertising campaign used in marketing. They work very well and do not allow customers to forget your brand. A cheaper option is to use promotional products. Combining these technologies can easily create a successful, trustworthy and attractive marketing strategy that adapts to your company’s advertising goals.

Final Thoughts

Your brand is an identity that your customers know. The importance of a brand identity cannot be underestimated. A brand is a plan for how customers perceive you and your company. It symbolizes the happiness, comfort, loyalty, and lasting impression of your brand. The company’s brand usually starts with a new company logo. Business reviews are basically the online opinions and comments that audience provide on your website or at the other platforms, about your services and business. Reviews depends upon how much they are liking your services how much they are satisfied with your services; they want to say something about your services.

Why online reviews are important?

Whether you’re liking it or not, your customers want to leave online reviews, and your prospective customers loves to them. Most of your new audience who come to review your business or wants to explore your business give a brief read to your reviews.  Some customers set their perspective upon reviews. You need to improve your brand

In today’s world customers will not hastily believe either you are offering high-quality services. They will believe that every business says that. Rank high on google

That means positive online reviews can get your business more qualified traffic and leads through Google search results pages. Reviews are especially important for local businesses that depend on walk-in customers. If you have zero or poor reviews, you can still get listed on the Google Map Pack.

Get more sales

Many people turn to their friends and family while looking for opinions and reviews about some product, business or services. So if your site or business are having the great you will get more traffic than you expect. And good recommendations by people always means a bigger and better sale.

Some tips to leverage your online business reviews

1. Take negative reviews about your services seriously

Don’t opposed or become defensive when you received a negative comment about your product. Use that comment as an improvement for your services, all you need to do is to response back to those comments and give them surety that you will address their issue. Reviews can identify all types of problem. If same reviews are coming or the same service than you should seriously look for that matter.

2. Share positive reviews in your marketing offers

Most of the customers will have a better view of your offerings and services when they are about reveal the positive reviews. For instance, if a customer gives you a positive review on one social media platform, then it’s time to feature it on other social media platforms as well. There is no harm in showing off, especially when it comes from a happy and satisfied customer.

3. Answer questions of you customers

Either you have small business or a large business you should get some time to answer your customer’s question. Giving response to your customer’s queries and satisfy them with their queries makes a great impact on your customers.

4. Make your website a review friendly

Review friendly means asking visitors to leave reviews and making it easy for them to do so. Make it easy for your customer to find where to add review, either they visiting on your website through systems or on mobile devices. Don’t ignore valid bad reviews, as these make your business more reliable. And you always need to response to the negative reviews in a positive and non-defensive way.

Your business will surely get benefits from those reviews!

Online reviews are not a new trend, but they are becoming more important from time to time. So, the best way to get positive reviews for your business is to provide great products and customer service. Far from that, it helps to find your customers and gently remind them to leave reviews.

Target the audience first according to the requirements of your niche and stay focus on the psychology they follow before making a sale. Then market your brand in a way that your message and best features of your product reach the maximum number of relevant audience. Prepare a  probability graph to calculate ROI of your plan and act accordingly.

Minimalism

Extra is not the trend of the market now. Minimalism is the phenomenon of pitching and presenting what is required only. Rather than wasting your time, money and energy on extra ornamentations its good to organize and manage what is important. From the name of your brand till the website design try to hold the approach of minimalism. Simple brand name, logo, packaging, website design, and advertisements are more impactful because they are easy to understand.

Utilize Maximum Resources

To market your brand and to add value to it, you can use several platforms at a time to hit the most number of targeted audience. It is not necessary to always focus on paid marketing and advertisements, your marketing plan must include free and easy assessable options as well. For example, social media account management, promotions, and sales for regular followers of the page, V-log, product tutorials etc. Try to utilize most of the available sources so that you can spread your brand more and more within less time.

Show Your Presence

Active participation to show your existence helps customers to trust your brand. You can set high positions in the market by grabbing people`s attention at events and celebrations. Your active presence on your social media pages, quick responses for queries of people and visibility in special events will make people think about your brand every time they will be buying certain products or services.

 

Hopefully, you will follow the mentioned techniques to build your brand more effectively and to expand your sales graph.

]]> Logo is the prime branding element for any company.
Your logo identifies your product. It is also considered as the core idea of the brand.
While designing logo, it is important to decide the shape, font and colors of the logo.
Once you build strong image of your brand, you can change your logo slightly but it takes time Your logo should not be adaptable, it has to be unique.

To design your logo hire logo designer who knows fundamental of graphic designing and also consumer behavior. An experience designer can help you in a professional way and consult you if your idea conflict with the audience’ perception.

Logo Adjustable in all Color Palette

Logo is the first impression of any brand. People look at the packaging and branding of product before buying it.
Use bright, bold and loud colors to take hold of consumers’ attention. Do not use flat colors, it can be adaptable easily. Choses color transitions of same color family to differentiate from other brands.
Using more than 2 colors distract viewers and their brand recall towards your brand. Use less but attractive colors to make your consumers remember it whenever they see your brand and they can relate your teaser campaigns to your brand easily. Before giving final look to your logo, put it on different color palettes to see if the logo is decipherable in all background colors. 

Broken Typography

Typography has a vast area to play with the letters. Break your letters in a creative way to give meaning to your brand. Make your viewer look twice and think about your brand later.
You can break A’s or straight letters to make and arrow and show the growth of your brand in this way.

Expressive textured letters

Use of serif and san serifs is important following any one form, illustrate letters according to the nature of your brand. If your brand nature is sweet and delicate, you can make it like that by soften the corners of letters. If you are a scuba driver, your logo could be water textured.
You can give horror look to the letters if your brand relate to some dangerous element.

Symmetrical letters

Patterns and repetition eliminates the chance of replication. Simple sleek letters are so mainstream, use geometrical letters to change the look and feel of your logo. Patterns create involvement of the viewers as they try to find the meaning behind it and are pleasant to eyes.
It also bring the homogeneity and empathy in all of your branding element.

Doodle your logo

Best way to create emotional attachment and uniqueness in your logo is to sketch it on the paper first.
Take a paper and draw whatever comes in your mind. Hand drawn logos are always unique and exclusive.

GIFs logo for web

2018 will be the era of moving pictures. Play puzzle with your logo. Make GIFS of your logo. Replace and rearrange tagline, object or text in the GIF.
GIFs and animation always attract customers and it brings a bit of entertainment for them.

Bring revolution in logo design by following the above creative ideas. Your logo deSignagency should know how to manipulate your logo according to the latest trend.
