Role of Explainer videos in E-commerce business

Explainer videos play a vital role in promoting your product or brand.
These videos describe about your brand personality, the benefits it provide along with how your brand provide solution to the problem your audience face.

Easy and rapid transaction

According to the survey, 37.5 million people in U.S transact from their phones.
Online shopping saves a lot of time. People prefer sitting at home and buy online rather than going out and visit every shop individually.
If you have an online payment system than you don’t have to doubt on your sales.

Short, sweet and alluring videos gains trust

Explainer videos are short and describes every aspect of your brand. By watching moving pictures of your product or service people tend to trust more on your brand.
As compare to pictures, explainer videos influence more online buyers. They couldn’t feel it but they know how exactly the product will be because through explainer video they can assume how the product would be in real. In explainer videos, you can also show the usage of your product and guide customers about the benefits of it.

Videos gain more traffic

Breakdown of the video should be done step by step.  From brand name to benefits, problem and its solution, and in the end, indirect action call. The more customized explainer video is the best explainer video to grasp more customers.

Successful explainer videos for business necessarily accommodate certain general traits that make them recognizable & acceptable among users such as understandable commentary, simple illustrations, discreet invitations and step-by-step guidance towards the usage of your web portal.

]]> Social media is undoubtedly the first way to interact with your customers these days. From Facebook to Instagram, from Snapchat to Twitter, there are several different social media websites where you can easily coordinate with your loved ones. Well, so do your customers. Your customers are present on any and every social media outlet there is. Well, when there’s audience on social media; so are brands.

For smaller brands, becoming the next big thing on social media is perhaps very easy and important as it can possibly be. It might sound very easy, but it’s very strategic and an important part will be played by the social media post designs. Most brands really don’t realize what they have to keep in mind with social media designs which is why we’re here to help.

Focus On The Agenda

Always make sure that the agenda of the social post is expressed beautifully through the designs. The design is undoubtedly one of the best things that you can work with. Never be blunt with your agenda in your social media designs. Express it in a subtle manner, make it the best that it can be. If you love aesthetics, play with it aesthetically. You should always love the way you work with your designs. If you don’t love and express your agenda via designs, it won’t really work for you either.

Include Your CTAs

Your CTAs or your call-to-action buttons won’t really seem like its important but without it you won’t be expecting any conversions either. Providing information to your customer is a great way to go but not informing them about the CTAs is a stupid decision. Here’s the thing, you have around a 5 second window to engage your customer and if your design doesn’t have a CTA, you won’t really be able to convert at all.

All in all, social designs are pretty important to work on if you’re searching for conversion based results. If there’s something that you want to add, you can easily do that without a problem by adding a comment below.
