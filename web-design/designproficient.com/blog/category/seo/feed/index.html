What Is SEO?

First, in order to improve your law firm’s search engine optimization, you need to know what it is. SEO stands for search engine optimization. It is a digital marketing technique that can increase website traffic, improving your ranking on search engines like Google and others. By optimizing your site, you can provide search engines and potential customers with clear information about who you are and what you do. This clarity leads to higher search engine rankings, which leads to more web traffic, more potential customers, and more customers.

Tips To Optimize Your Law Firm Website With SEO

Always Use Relevant Keywords

In order for your keywords to come to work, you need to make sure they are placed appropriately on the entire home page. Add keywords to titles, headings, and home page content. It is best to include the keywords with which you want to get the highest ranking in the title—for example, the central business area. Therefore, you need to use a “Phoenix DUI Lawyer” instead of an “Arizona Lawyer.”

Content Creation

You should regularly create new and relevant content that supports your keyword strategies. Optimize the content of web pages and blog posts and the corresponding background tags. A web page includes a home page, but it does not end there. For each service, you offer, create high quality, optimized service page, and a general service login page with links to each more specific service page.

You also must create an “About Us” page to attract the attention of site visitors. The contact information on each page facilitates contact. Google has a “freshness” rating mark so that it can reward sites with frequently updated content, and blogs are the ideal way to do this. In addition, blog posts can be used to target more search-based keywords.

Make Sure Your Site Is Responsive & Mobile-friendly

Everyone uses smartphones today, and some people use smartphones instead of PCs to surf the internet. You want your site to be mobile-friendly so people can use it anytime, anywhere. When someone is seeking a lawyer, they wait patiently until the site loads on the phone or does not respond. This is a surefire way to lose potential customers. In addition, mobile-friendly sites are also an aspect of search engine optimization. Search engines like Google use responsive web design as a critical factor in ranking sites.

Improve Website Speed & Keep It Up To Date

We are less patient than ever, and there is more information on the internet than ever. This means that when we have an unpleasant experience on any website, we are there! Having a fast-loading website is important for potential customers to stay and return to your website, as well as ranking your website on the search engine.

Make sure that all extensions are up to date. Reduce the size of images on a website. Minimize the number of HTTP requests needed to upload images, graphics, etc. to a page. Save and send the image in JPEGS format, it loads much faster than PNGS.

You don’t want to waste time researching keywords, developing a blog, and checking the speed of sites, so you spend months or even years. You need to make sure that your site is full of new content, links are not broken, and extensions are up to date. To continue to rank in SEO and attract new visitors, you must regularly review the content and performance of your website.

Online Presence On Other Law Firm Platforms

Yes, you also need to take care of your online status, not only your website but on other sites too. But this can bring optimized search engine optimization to your law firm’s website. If you have personal information on a website like NEXL, make sure that your personal information is displayed correctly, updated, and optimized. They can be updated according to discipline or location, and the content of these types of sites matches the content of your site, which can help to rank your site.

One Of The Most Important Parts Of SEO “Link Building”

Based on your SEO campaign, you need more high-quality inbound links, that is, links to your website that point to your website. If your link profile is healthy, Google will trust your site and reward you as a result. These links must come from related sites across different sets of domains. One way to expand links is to share content. You can share your content through social media, email marketing, and more. The proportion of persons who read your content will link to you.

Another option is to publish your website on a free or paid quality list and create a specific website for your law firm. If it is listed in the directory, check that the NAP is correct. Also, consider the member organization to which you belong (regardless of region, state, or country). These organizations publish and link law firms on your websites.

Make Sure Your Pages Are Indexed

If the page is not indexed, it means that the page will not appear in the expected search. This is bad for most pages, particularly the home page, the about us and the service pages. To find out the number of pages indexed, you can use a tool called “Website Auditor.” You can grasp the number of pages indexed using various online tools. You need a number close to the actual number of pages on your site. If there are any deficiencies, you can also hire SEO Experts to manage your website pages.

Conclusion

The lawyer’s website needs to be loaded as quickly as possible to maintain rankings and get more visitors and new prospects. If you are familiar with developing websites from the back-end, you are already one step ahead. In many cases, lawyers found that they didn’t have time to focus on customizing the site during the day of the trial. In that case, consider hiring experienced professionals to perform these tasks.

]]> Google ranking is the fluctuating position of your website in the search engine result page which determines the success and failure of your website. It is not always possible to retain a stable ranking position for a longer time as it always fluctuates upward and downward.

It is necessary for you to have a nicely built search engine friendly website by complying with proper search marketing strategies to help your website retain the 1st-page ranking.

In this blog post, we will discuss the 8 things which do not directly affect your Google ranking.

ALSO READ: Boost Your Business Revenue With These 3 Steps

Website age

Website age is an essential search engine optimization factor which does not directly affect your ranking.  The older age registration of your website domain does provide a reliable source of authority and trust for Google. However, the necessary things to do are quality relevant backlink profile which increases the value of the website with original quality content which drives the attention of readers.

Social sharing

Social sharing links also do not affect your Google ranking. As Google keeps no concerns with your increasing number of shares, likes, and tweet counts. However, social sharing does help in boosting valuable traffic for your website. Social sharing helps in building a strong brand portfolio for your website to reach more audience with an increase in reputation.

Bounce rate

Bounce rate is the stepping back of the visitor from your website. It spoils the reputation of your website in Google. The higher bounce rate leads to a lower click-through rate with a decrease in website traffic. The good or bad bounce rate does not affect your website ranking. It is suitable for your website to sustain visitors by producing quality engaging content with more dwell time.

Heading tags

The heading tags are HTML tags which comprise of six varieties of tags ranging from h1 to h6. The <h1> is the most substantial heading tag and <h6> is the smallest heading tags in your website. These tags do not affect your website ranking but are necessary for showing the website status.

Robots.txt and XML sitemap

The robots.txt is Meta source file which has a purpose of restricting the bots to crawl the specific pages in your website. XML sitemap provides the fast crawling and indexing of your website pages to let know Google about them. These files do not affect your ranking of the website but are essential factors for SEO.

Meta keywords

Meta keywords are the generic list of HTML keywords which help to show up ranking for your website. The use of these Meta keywords is not much recommended in modern SEO, and they also do not much affect your website ranking.

Alt Image Keywords

The purpose of the alt image tag is to provide ranking by labeling a keyword title to your website images. The alt image keywords also do not affect much to your website ranking.

Schema markup

Schema markup is an ideal technique to boost up enhanced and dynamic search listing for your website. It better works for local businesses, book reviews, movies, and restaurants. The use of schema markup also does not affect your website ranking.

It has been found that proposing not more than two products in one place always translates into higher sales. Offering one product is ideal for one target market. And It is better to focus on one product and answers all the queries of your customers related to that product. Instead of trying to gratify your site visitors by giving them a large variety of products with less information about each service or product.

 

2.    Keep Up your website with Customers

Do you know what’s more to focus on your website? It is your customers. The most effective sales copy emphases on its customers or reader. Unfortunately, many businesspersons do not give importance to this simple golden rule. Be very careful about your sales copy. See if it is filled with references to “I,” “me” and “we”? It is always better to use “you” and “your” in content.  By so doing, you are assuring customer’s importance and making content customer-centric and increase sales on site.

 

3.    Search engines love Fresh web-content

Fresh, good web content helps to gain trustworthiness, reliance and ultimately sales from the people who visit your website. Start with brainstorming, see how you can make your web content more useful. Make sure your site looks professional, fresh and genuine. It must include your mission statement and possess relevant information about you and your services. The best web content should be user-centric. It should offer the information your visitors pursue and to present it in engaging and organized manner.

Here are some effective tips to help make your web content fresh and good:

  • Use conversational Language.
  • Use acquainted terminologies.
  • Offer all the relevant information.
  • Prefer real photos for your site. They work best.
  • Explain why your business is uniquely equipped to offer its products or services.
  • Keep your web content always up-to-date
  • Mention your contact information in multiple places
  • Clearly explain to your site visitors what you would like them to do next
  • Proofread your content. See, if it’s free from grammatical mistakes.

 

4.    Get More Sales with Social Networking

Having great content on website is good. But, it is not enough to produce high content and make the expectation that visitors find it – you have to control a whole situation rather than just responding to it after any mishap has happened. You have to get social to boost website traffic. Use different social media channels to promote your content. With 1.4 billion users, Facebook is ideal for interacting with customers. Twitter has more than 300 million, is renowned as a momentous brand awareness driving force. Pinterest is a giant in attracting predominantly female audiences. Instagram is popular among the young audience.

No matter what social media channel you’re opting for, your web content must always be geared toward reaching those visitors who can become potential customers or true fan.

5.    SEO can never get old.

The practice is valuable and worth more than before. There are two types of SEO: on-page and off-page. The techniques like creating internal links to your content, making the most of image alt text, giving importance to Meta descriptions are still considered as the backbone of On-page SEO. They help boost your organic traffic. On the other hand, off page SEO techniques can be used to improve the position of a website in the SERPs. The techniques include: Contribute as Guest Author, Forum submissions, Blog Directory Submission, Article Submission, Influencer Outreach and the list is endless.

6.    Keywords still matter

If you want more traffic, more leads, and better sales, then you’ll have to learn the proper use of keyword optimization to get more business. In keywords, you have two choices: Short-tail keywords and long-tail keywords. Short tail keywords are search watchwords with only one or two words. Long-tail keywords are broader, have three or more words.  Long tail keywords are more targeted and have significant search volume. Before going to actual keyword research, take some quality time to ask a few questions to yourself:

  • Who is my audience?
  • Type of content my readers want to read
  • What should I offer as a solution for my customers?

You can also take help with keyword tools and get hundreds of keyword suggestions for your site, personalized to your industry and place.

The Knowledge Graph

Since the establishment, The Knowledge Graph has been getting more popularity. Google is using the Knowledge Graph to enhance its search engine’s services with information collected from a variety of sources. Moreover, this refers to Google’s central database of information on movies, historical events, locations, and other topics; when a user searches any topic, a box on the right-side of the search engine results page will display relevant information to that topic along with links to Google pages for other related topics.
