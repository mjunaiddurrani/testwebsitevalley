]]> Human errors can ruin the whole concept and scenario of any design. When it comes to website designing, the situation is even more critical. When a website or illustration on a website is viewed over a big screen, whether you want it or not human errors can come under limelight automatically.

Nowadays most websites are designed over any specified theme. This trend makes the job of designers even more difficult. One single error can be a cause of rejection of design from your clients because with the very little mistake the whole theme get disturbed.

Being a human we cannot avoid errors and there is no harm in making errors until they become a cause of rejection of your design. Here are some tips through which one can easily find human errors in a website design.

Zoom In

When you create a mock-up or before presenting the idea to your client, check every detail by zooming in and zooming out the design again and again. Zooming option allows you to view every minor and major detail in your design properly. This cross-check will help you to sort out things before client`s eye can catch them.

Colors in Number

Depending upon the quality of monitor/screen and technology that a designer is using the display of colors can vary. Sometimes the difference is of high level that it can cause major issues for any design. They can affect the aesthetic appeal and disturb the theme. To avoid such issues it is suggested to add colors by noticing the numerical figure assigned for every tone of color in software or shade card.

Grids

Even if you are making a grid-free design for the website, you have to follow certain scales to make your design effective, appealing and organized. Following grinds or aligning the design elements with reference to lines can be helpful to detect orientation and placement errors in any design.

Take reviews

Showing your design to multiple people and allowing them to criticize openly is a good practice. Firstly, it will help you to improve. Secondly, your design navigation will be tested practically. Thirdly, you can learn psychological tactics to deal people with designing. Fourthly, you will be learning to counter errors or concepts of your design.

Test before Going Live

 

Test the design, interface, navigation, color scheme and functionality of website on several devices to make sure that it is working properly and everything is just as accurate as you wished them to be. Test them on different IOS,  as well as on cell phones, tablets, PCs, Gaming PCs, Smart TVs etc. because sometimes varying the view from different devices can be helpful to find out your design errors.

Hopefully, you find my blog knowledgeable and helpful. Follow mentioned techniques to increase the proficiency and accuracy of your design so that you can learn new things and can present best in front of your clients.
