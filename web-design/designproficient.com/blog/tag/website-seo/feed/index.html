]]> Launching a website is the only half route towards online business, the other half is to bring potential customers to your website and stop the audience from driving away. Many small budget and simple sites can keep customers intact whereas many high budget websites are a total flop. The reason lies within the website design and development.

Here are six ways of driving customers away from a website and if your website is following any such pattern it’s time to change to earn more and more happy customers.

ALSO READ: How to make money as a Graphic Designer?

URL is too complicated or misleading

Complicated URLs never let the audience find your website while searching something of Google. URLs with numbers or tricky name, confusing spelling considered as least professional or tacky. Misleading URLs also play a role in confusing clients, and they don’t click BUY NOW button.

Haphazard Landing Page

Landing page with incorrect and unclear information is the biggest reason for leaving a website right ahead without checking out further information and company`s message. Landing page image and video are the two best chances to attract visitors and provoke them to visit service page or inventory of any website. When images are unorganized, information is not worthy, and the content is chaotic the visitors leave the site within 10 to 15 seconds.

Pop-ups

One or two pop-ups are fine, but too many pop-ups are exasperating and annoying. Useless and irrelevant pop-ups will drag the customer’s attention to something else, and many of them will shift to other websites.

Grammar Errors in Content

Grammar errors show the unprofessionalism and non-serious attitude of your company. Write simple and straightforward content with no grammar issues rather than finding quality in complex and long sentences.

Complex Vocabulary

No one will pay time to check for synonyms before placing an order on a website that’s why complex and rarely used vocabulary will only irritate people by not letting them understand your message. With poor message deliverance, the chances of getting good business are not possible.

Auto-Music or Video

Believe it or not auto music and video is a real pain. Firstly, out of nowhere something starts to play no matter you want to watch it or not. Secondly, it increases loading time, and in case of slow internet people can’t scroll or check other pages because of auto pay mode. Usually among multiple tabs, if something unnecessarily starts playing something people without giving a single look to website close that particular tab.

The kind of content required for search engine optimization has to qualify certain algorithms that Google announces in order to escalate a website’s ranking on the internet. Let us discuss a few below!

Keep your page load low – According to famous digital marketer and SEO expert, Neil Patel says that 40% of the people withdraw themselves from surfing on a page that takes more than 3 seconds to load. Make sure you craft the pages keeping these stats in mind.

Make powerful and meaningful headlines – Headlines play an important role for SEO content. First of all your content must include headlines and these headlines should be in correct length and include relevant keywords in them.

1st tip# Create great content

If you want to rank high in the search engine, then a high content can help you the most. Websites with a fresh, genuine and relevant content are more likely to get a chance to rank. Avoid grammatical mistakes and spelling lower. The question, how long the content should be? must be popping in your mind. Well, there’s no exact answer to it, but it should be at least not smaller than 500 words. In fact, for many, it is good minimum length for a typical blog post.

If we go with research, then it has shown that posts longer than 1000 words tend to rank better. Search engine likes authoritative posts with more than 2000 words. It means, if you want to improve your ranking chances in the future with in-depth articles or blogs, you have to focus on both quality and quantity. The content must be excellent in quality, should be more than 2000 words. So, try to make your writings fun to read, avoid being too much promotional, don’t be boring and assure your content should not look like a legal document. As an alternative, opt for friendly, amusing content.

2nd tip# Build backlinks to your website

You need to link your web content with other internal custom web pages. This would be beneficial to your audience, will help improve your website’s SEO, make it comfortable for readers to find something useful from your site.

3rd tip# Optimized URLs

It will benefit you more if you put keywords in URLs.  Make sure, the keywords you’re putting should be relevant and not inadequate.

4th tip# Page titles

Your website’s page must possess compelling and relevant title with some keywords. The title should be descriptive enough to grasp by the readers about the content. For instance, if your blog is about “Effective Logo Designing,” then a great title should be “Effective Logo Designing in ten easy tips” or “How to create an effective logo for your business.” However, make sure that your page title should not longer than 70 characters. Although Google will index lengthier page titles, they will be shortened in the search results.

5th tip# Header tags

It would be perfect to incorporate the keyword phrase in a header tag, but make sure you’re not spoiling it. The ideal way to include a keyword phrase in a header tag is to use the first heading on the web page as the search engine will pay more attention to H1.

 

 

6th tip# Make your Professional Web page load faster

For search engine how much time your website takes in load matters a lot. In fact, it is ranking factor for Google. New case study sheds light that when search engine added website speed into the Algorithm, sites that took more than five minutes to load had to face a severe drop in traffic from search engine. That means fixing page load speed is necessary which can be done by:

  • Check how much time your website takes to load.
  • Optimize your website or web page to improve the overall performance of it.
  • If you’re using a shared hosting server, you can try switching to a different hosting provider to see if it makes a positive difference. Measure performance again after making changes.
  • See if your website has any unnecessary things, remove all of them quickly.

1.    One Brand, One Product, One Target Market

If your website is selling a wide range of products and services, then must test whether this strategy is effective for making more sales or not. It has been found that proposing not more than two products in one place always translates into higher sales. Offering one product is ideal for one target market. And It is better to focus on one product and answers all the queries of your customers related to that product. Instead of trying to gratify your site visitors by giving them a large variety of products with less information about each service or product.

 

2.    Keep Up your website with Customers

Do you know what’s more to focus on your website? It is your customers. The most effective sales copy emphases on its customers or reader. Unfortunately, many businesspersons do not give importance to this simple golden rule. Be very careful about your sales copy. See if it is filled with references to “I,” “me” and “we”? It is always better to use “you” and “your” in content.  By so doing, you are assuring customer’s importance and making content customer-centric and increase sales on site.

 

3.    Search engines love Fresh web-content

Fresh, good web content helps to gain trustworthiness, reliance and ultimately sales from the people who visit your website. Start with brainstorming, see how you can make your web content more useful. Make sure your site looks professional, fresh and genuine. It must include your mission statement and possess relevant information about you and your services. The best web content should be user-centric. It should offer the information your visitors pursue and to present it in engaging and organized manner.

Here are some effective tips to help make your web content fresh and good:

  • Use conversational Language.
  • Use acquainted terminologies.
  • Offer all the relevant information.
  • Prefer real photos for your site. They work best.
  • Explain why your business is uniquely equipped to offer its products or services.
  • Keep your web content always up-to-date
  • Mention your contact information in multiple places
  • Clearly explain to your site visitors what you would like them to do next
  • Proofread your content. See, if it’s free from grammatical mistakes.

 

4.    Get More Sales with Social Networking

Having great content on website is good. But, it is not enough to produce high content and make the expectation that visitors find it – you have to control a whole situation rather than just responding to it after any mishap has happened. You have to get social to boost website traffic. Use different social media channels to promote your content. With 1.4 billion users, Facebook is ideal for interacting with customers. Twitter has more than 300 million, is renowned as a momentous brand awareness driving force. Pinterest is a giant in attracting predominantly female audiences. Instagram is popular among the young audience.

No matter what social media channel you’re opting for, your web content must always be geared toward reaching those visitors who can become potential customers or true fan.

5.    SEO can never get old.

The practice is valuable and worth more than before. There are two types of SEO: on-page and off-page. The techniques like creating internal links to your content, making the most of image alt text, giving importance to Meta descriptions are still considered as the backbone of On-page SEO. They help boost your organic traffic. On the other hand, off page SEO techniques can be used to improve the position of a website in the SERPs. The techniques include: Contribute as Guest Author, Forum submissions, Blog Directory Submission, Article Submission, Influencer Outreach and the list is endless.

6.    Keywords still matter

If you want more traffic, more leads, and better sales, then you’ll have to learn the proper use of keyword optimization to get more business. In keywords, you have two choices: Short-tail keywords and long-tail keywords. Short tail keywords are search watchwords with only one or two words. Long-tail keywords are broader, have three or more words.  Long tail keywords are more targeted and have significant search volume. Before going to actual keyword research, take some quality time to ask a few questions to yourself:

  • Who is my audience?
  • Type of content my readers want to read
  • What should I offer as a solution for my customers?

You can also take help with keyword tools and get hundreds of keyword suggestions for your site, personalized to your industry and place.

What does SEO friendly website really mean? Simply it is a website that is easy to read and understand by the search engines. SEO friendly website is actually a visitor friendly website.

Some of the important characteristics of SEO friendly websites are:

  • To be responsive
  • To be not too fussy or ornate to read
  • Should be User-friendly
  • Should be mobile-responsive
  • Should possess optimized images with the text
  • Having clear hierarchy and text-based Navigation Structure

  1. Responsiveness

Responsiveness is a key to make your web page look appealing on all browsers and devices- desktop, mobile phone, and, tablet. Responsive design has three principles to be executed to work properly: Fluid grids, Media queries, and flexible images. To be responsive actually means to be adaptable.

  1. Optimizing Images

Another way to make your site SEO friendly is to optimize images with texts. Just name your images descriptively, optimize your alt attributes, choose image dimensions, optimize thumbnails, use image sitemaps and test your images.

In fact, there’re many compression tools available that make the art of image optimization possible. ImageRecycle, OptimiZilla, Tinypng, giftofspeed, and kraken are some of the examples to compress multiple images or files in one go.

  1. Videos and Infographics

Info graphics are  a visual representation of data beneficial to the website. Because more than 85% of information transmitted to brain is visual.

  1. Great UX Leads to High Ranking

Users are prior and good user experience help your site to convert more. If users coming your site find any distractions and difficulty, they’ll abandon. On the other hand, site having appealing look, intuitiveness, easy to navigate with clear instructions get more chance to engage users.

  1. Make your URL’s User Friendly

It is not only your responsive web layout or good content but also URL that should be user friendly. It is easy to read and understand. User friendly URL help people to remember the web address, increase click through rate and even influence social metrics.

Just keep it short, use relevant keywords, use real words, be descriptive, and not to prefer using tracking parameters.

At the end, these five elements are enough to make your site SEO friendly. But there are many other things you can do, like:

  • Incorporating social media elements into your website
  • Add 2-5 relevant keywords to the single web page
  • Use JavaScript in restricted manner, do not use it for whole site design
  • Do not use too much flashy elements, it can be distracting
  • Create page hierarchy properly
  • Write unique titles and meta descriptions
  • Reduce Code bloat, HTML table Usage
  • Lessen the number of on-page links
  • Implement proper heading tag and structured data
  • Use properly Coded lists
  • Keep an eye on your security certificate renewals
