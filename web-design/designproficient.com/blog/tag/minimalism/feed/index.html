]]> Your site ranking is important for your business and it is not only dependent of keywords but there are several other factors as well. Adding relevant keyword is as crucial as using coding for building layout. But the best use of keywords can only benefit when your site follows other specifications of SEO as well.

Following are the tips you must know for effective SEO tips to rank a beginner website.

ALSO READ: 5 Brilliant Tips for Animated Motion Graphics Video

Choose Specific Topic for Site

Pick any one topic and try to stick with it.  You cannot appeal every other person. The same goes for the website. Therefore, the designer should focus on targeted audience and one topic. Think about your website’s purpose, choose the specific topic, set target audience demographics and make your site accordingly.

Use Keywords Wisely

Keywords are the king, serves the important purpose. But the wise use of keyword can only help you with your site ranking. That particular thing of your site (the topic of the site) should be mentioned where they matter; in title and description. The designer should also add keywords not only in texts but on the images. H1, H2, H3 are just not headings but have other functions as well. They can change the game of keyword settings.

Add Backlinks to the page

Link-building is another strategy that works. You should add links of other relevant pages on your web page. Adding backlinks to the page help you increase the site rankings.

Avoid Unnecessary Content

Delays in page loading can be harmful to the site because if users find any difficulty in loading they will quickly switch to any other website. So, while designing a website just be careful about the addition of non-essential things. Enormous visual content, too much content and even unnecessary plugins can be a reason for the delay while loading a website.

Update Regularly

Update your site frequently because if your website’s content is getting older then it is also losing a chance of ranking. Most of the companies update their sites just to ensure if they get a better chance of ranking.

Fresh content is a fuel of the site, which not only renews your site but also works well for social media as people want to read and share new content and eventually benefits SEO.

Use Genuine and Quality Content

The designer should prefer quality content instead of stealing others material. Stealing content is easy and a quick way to start a business website but it can ruin your investment as well. Copied content is not SEO friendly and Google marks it as spam.

Add unique articles or blogs, use the interactive visual content to appeal visitors. Even site with unique content is 50% more likely to get chance of getting more visitors than the site without genuine content.

 

Make your site Easy-to-navigate for the visitors

Minimalism and simplicity are the two things that designer should keep in mind while designing. If your site is simple, users will find no difficulty in navigation. Define each category or sub-category clearly for the users. Add ‘Call to action’ to the site – a phrase that eases viewers to take immediate action, it could be; call us, message us, add to the cart, subscribe now.

Forget black hat SEO and Prefer White hat SEO

Use white hat SEO tactics if you want your site to be rank for long on the Google. Even using tactics like article spinning, reporting competitors’ page considered as an illegal, against to the SEO rules that surely ruin your site.

 

Building a website that stand-alone in the market is a dream of every new designer. But it is not just the matter of knowing few coding, rather you also have to focus on Site’s SEO too.

]]> Being a business owner or a marketer it is your utmost priority to build a value by associating all good with your brand’s name. But it is not a simple task.  Strong brand image leads to the popularity of the brand and ultimately towards better sales. The competition in the market is high and to stand out you need to follow some smart tactics.

Following I have mentioned some point that can help you build your brand smartly and effectively for proficient results.

ALSO READ: 3 Things To Keep In Mind When Hiring A Web Design Company

 SWOT

Create a plan before pitching anything to your clients. Your small mistake can ruin your image in the market and can make you suffer a lot. SWOT analysis of your idea can help you understand the strength and weaknesses. You can calculate the risk of failure and can redesign your strategy for better results.

Target Audience

Keeping an eye on your goal is the right way to stand in the market. Target the audience first according to the requirements of your niche and stay focus on the psychology they follow before making a sale. Then market your brand in a way that your message and best features of your product reach the maximum number of relevant audience. Prepare a  probability graph to calculate ROI of your plan and act accordingly.

Minimalism

Extra is not the trend of the market now. Minimalism is the phenomenon of pitching and presenting what is required only. Rather than wasting your time, money and energy on extra ornamentations its good to organize and manage what is important. From the name of your brand till the website design try to hold the approach of minimalism. Simple brand name, logo, packaging, website design, and advertisements are more impactful because they are easy to understand.

Utilize Maximum Resources

To market your brand and to add value to it, you can use several platforms at a time to hit the most number of targeted audience. It is not necessary to always focus on paid marketing and advertisements, your marketing plan must include free and easy assessable options as well. For example, social media account management, promotions, and sales for regular followers of the page, V-log, product tutorials etc. Try to utilize most of the available sources so that you can spread your brand more and more within less time.

Show Your Presence

Active participation to show your existence helps customers to trust your brand. You can set high positions in the market by grabbing people`s attention at events and celebrations. Your active presence on your social media pages, quick responses for queries of people and visibility in special events will make people think about your brand every time they will be buying certain products or services.

 

Hopefully, you will follow the mentioned techniques to build your brand more effectively and to expand your sales graph.
