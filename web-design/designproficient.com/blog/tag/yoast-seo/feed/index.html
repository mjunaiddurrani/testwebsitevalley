]]> Nowadays, every professional knows the importance of Search engine optimization (SEO) tools. The right tool can help you ease the difficult task of optimizing a site for higher rankings.

Below are five important tools every SEO industry should use using to increase their productivity:

Ahrefs

One of the best toolsets for search engine optimizations, covers competitor analysis, backlink checking, batch analysis, keyword research, comprehensive backlinks Report and many more thing you and your business need to know. It allows you explore website comprehensively and also check backlinks for providing SEO data. Ahrefs is a well-known tool that runs SEO reports on URL or domain and provides key metrics on numerous referring pages/domains/IPs/ and anchor pages.

Google Search Console

Even, today many people get confused about Google search console and think it same as Google Analytics. Both are different and good at the same time. The Google Search Console is a tool help you rank in Google’s search results and can be used for analyzing site’s search ranking, backlinks, search clicks and more. It focuses on site’s visibility in search engines.  On the other hand, Google Analytics offers insight into your website’s traffic and how your site visitors use it.

Authority Labs

One of the key tasks of SEO is to monitor rankings on a regular basis. For this purpose, Authority lab is a reliable tool to monitor a daily SEO ranking and also automates competitor tracking, campaign monitoring, local rank tracking and many more. Unlike other traditional tools, it is not time-consuming or asks your precious money. It is the free and worthy tool to obtain keyword rankings for your clients and their competitor. All you have to do is add the keywords and domains you want to monitor, and that’s it!

The tool offers you daily rank checking, tracks search results for accurate reporting, and also adds domains or pages from any country and language offered by the search engine. With this amazing tool, you have the best opportunity to react to changes in the search results, track competitors’ domains,

Yoast SEO

Yoast SEO is an ideal tool if your website is powered by WordPress. Just download and install this undisputed leader. It just works as an Army knife of SEO plugins, together with features like XML sitemap creation, title and Meta tag customization, canonical URL creation, keyword targeting and more.

Canva.Com

Last but not the least, Canva is the latest tool is used by many SEO industries now. The tool is powerful and well-equipped in building content quickly. With more than million users, it provides access to over a million photographs, graphics, and fonts. The best thing about this is, it is free and easy to use.

So, these are some of the important tools used by many SEO industries, today.
