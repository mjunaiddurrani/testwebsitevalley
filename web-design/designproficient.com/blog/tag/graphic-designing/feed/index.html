]]> Making money as a Graphic Designer is not everyone’s cup of tea. Graphic designing may seem an easy and effortless profession but the truth isn’t that smooth. It requires talent and perfect promotion both to earn a living as a graphic designer. Graphic designing is strictly a creative and artistic skill and people who are lost in art, usually are not that proactive with selling their art to the right people in the right way. Most artists eventually get underestimated due to lack of promotion and PR. If you are one of those graphic artists who is looking for ways to extend their income through correct routes then this blog might save you some efforts to capitalize your skill.

ALSO READ: How much to charge clients as a new designer?

Pursue a Graphic Designing Degree

Graphic Designing is more of a skill and mostly people earn this skill through various courses and proper training to get their hands on tools. There are many institutions around the world which offer legitimate degree programs on graphic designing. We recommend you to earn a degree if you are skilled enough with designing to make your profile more professional. This makes it easier for you to get hired by good companies.

Build an impressive Portfolio

Create an account on any online portfolio websites such as Behance and add your best pieces of work there. An impressive portfolio plays a convincing role to get more clients online and this is going to be more impactful than your resume or CV. So if you don’t yet have a maintained portfolio, now is the time to start with it.

Be a Freelancer

Freelancing is the most practiced mode of money making by graphic designers. Freelance websites like Upwork and Fiverr has given a privilege of earning in millions to many individuals through high convenience and guaranteed clients. All you would need to take care of will be keep yourself away from scammers who don’t pay after getting projects done.

Sell your files online

Create your best pieces of digital artwork and you can sell them on multiple platforms online. If you are okay with selling away your copyrights then you can be one of those millionaires who mint money through online work!

Earning money is not an easy task until you find the hack of getting into the right direction. Discovering your skills is important but selling them is also very important!

]]> User Interface and User Experience are the two terminologies that hold the criteria of good or bad designing in the world of websites. The more user-friendly design is, the more benefits it caters. The major reasons for making design simple and easy to make it understandable. Also, people want a website that guides them with necessary points in an easy yet convincing way.

ALSO READ: 3 Reasons Why 2018 Is the Year for Video Content Engagement

More Views More Sales

The minimal term refers to simplicity in design. The phenomena of removing all unnecessary and decorative elements to make the website design sophisticated and user-friendly is being the current trend. The reason designers are incorporating it into their work is that consumers are found to view such websites more. More views and more exploration on the website directly lead to more sales.

We can say that minimalism approach in designing can make the process of sales easier and faster and this is all that a company wants.

Time-Saving for Designers and Developers

Another benefit of minimalism in web designing is the time-saving factor. The whole process of designing and development is much easier when with minimalism approach in designing is adopted. Today the ongoing trend is of customized website designing so design houses or individual designers cannot pay hours and hours to one project hence they minimalism provides them ease. The design is functional and easily operated that it is proficient for customers.

Quick look, Quick Decision

Previously people use to spend hours on the decorative and extra elements, at that time this approach was fascinating because there were limited websites active for any specific service or product but now the competition is tough and people have a lot of options when they search for anything. In such circumstances people open a website, give it a quick look, if they find it easy going or understandable they pay some time to it otherwise they quickly shift to other websites.

For such quick look, quick decision nature of consumers, it is ideal to present things in a way that they get the brand`s message at first glimpse.

Highlight what is Important

As the website has selective things it is easy for designers to bring out important things to the front. The simple tactics like changing the size of the font or adding bright colors to key points grab the attention of viewers quickly. Also, consumers find the specific website, easy to explore because they don’t have to dig out required information from a junk of content. Because it highlights what is most important it actually helps users to operate the website easily and somehow convince them to buy mentioned products or services.

 

Those were some the reason why we think the minimal design is the future of web UI/UX. The most important point is that audience is loving such designs. For a brand as well as for a designer this is the most considerable point of adopting minimalism design approach in future.
