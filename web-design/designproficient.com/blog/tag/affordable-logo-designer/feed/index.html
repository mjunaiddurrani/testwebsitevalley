]]> The logo doesn’t need any introduction as a business recognition. It is a small figurative mark of your business that gives your business a ‘recognition’. Holding a recognition of business, the importance of well-designed logo cannot be neglected. History is packed with numerous famous logo designs and their success stories. If we take an example of our very own Nike’s Logo, we all know the company’s logo is famous for its simplicity and minimal approach that has taken a design to the next level. It is simple, easy to understand, easy to recall, and one of the best examples of ‘Less is more’ approach. We can find a number of designs just like Nike who are known for their unique design. Some have incorporated minimal principle, and some are the exemplification of maximalism principle. Which principle should be used depends on your business nature, however, it is important for each design to stand out and be noticeable among the array of other logo design. In fact, this is how your logo can succeed and so may your business to grab maximum eyeballs and ultimately makes more sales or increases ROI. The study reveals that your logo designs directly impacts the customer purchasing behavior. It is one of the things that helps people all across the world to understand your business nature, brand’s offerings, business value or worth. With such immense significance, one cannot say ‘NO’ to its importance. Making these small figurative marks as noticeable as much possible is the only way to get your business message out to the mass. But, that needs a lot of struggle, consideration, and witty approach. Don’t worry, I have taken some of the most important tips out from the experts for you. So, without any further ado. Go ahead:

1.  Go With Plan

Always go with planning. Pre-planned things are more likely to succeed than unplanned things. Make sure you are planning each thing properly. How your logo should be, what customers will be its targeting, what message it will convey, how flexible it should be, and there are countless things need to be planned.

2.  Don’t Fall For Premade Custom Logos, Hire an Expert

That’s completely fine if you know not much about logo designing since you are an entrepreneur, not a designer. There’s always a good alternative. Hire an expert designer who knows basic to advance principles of designs, color psychology, and possesses years of experience in the reputable organization.

Commonly, many people have been seen going for a premade logos. Premade or ready-made logos are not appropriate for your business. They are not unique and so should not be used.

3.  Don’t Complicate, Make It Simple

Things if not simple, will sucks – to you and to your customers who are speculating on your logo and have lots of expectations from you. It is not good in any way to make them angry and confused. In fact, the purpose of the logo is to convey brand’s message. If they are multifaceted, they will not serve their primary goal.

4.  Avoid Unnecessariness, Incorporate Only Needed Things

This point is an extension of them –. With simplicity, it meant to incorporate only things your logo needs or you think essential to add. Take a paper and pen out. Write about your business—what your business is about, who your customers are, what they do, and all. Write five two-three lines on each and then summarize. Hand over this summary to your designer and ask him to incorporate them as much as possible. Heavy ornaments, loud color tones, excessive text are the things can make your design meager.

5.  Get Some Inspiration

The present market is overflowing with a number of good-to-inspire logo designs. Why not take some inspiration and take a hold of the good things from them? Research the market, get some good logo designs to see, study them and take inspiration for your coming business logo. By inspiration, it doesn’t mean to steal their work.

Last Thought

The logo is crucial for your business. Make it stand out and noticeable. Make your business well-known in the eyes of the public and get more sales. Directly or indirectly, it all starts with your logo, so don’t compromise with its eminence.

For designing a logo, it is important to understand certain things. Every single line, shape, color, and form are important in making a conscious decision when it comes to design a logo for your business.

SO, WHAT IS FIGURE-GROUND RELATIONSHIP IN CUSTOM LOGO FOR YOUR BUSINESS?

Figure and ground are two terms with different meanings. The figure refers to the positive space in the logo design. Whereas, ground refers to the background or negative space in the emblem. Commonly, a logo is visually prevailing in the figure. Sometimes, the elements of the figure are ground dominating instead of being figure dominant.

WHY THE CONCEPT IS IMPORTANT FOR CUSTOM LOGO DESIGNERS?

When it comes to the importance of Figure-Ground relationship in logos, look Gestalt Principle. It will tell you that design is perceived as a unified whole. Figure-Ground Relationship is an extension of the Gestalt Principle.

When people view a logo, they see individual elements and categorize it in relationships. It means negative space is equally important as positive space because both spaces exist in relation and depend on each other.

KINDS OF FIGURE GROUND RELATIONSHIPS:

There are three types of figure-ground relationships. These are:

1.    Constant Figure-Ground Relationship:

This type allows a person to differentiate a ground and figure in the logo design easily. For making logo stable enough, the designer has to make one thing dominant over other. For instance, they can make negative space more prominent over positive space in the design.

2.    Reversible Figure-Ground Relationship

Both figure and the ground get equal attention in this type. In such design, there is space for the ground to take over the figure and for the figure to take over the ground. By doing so, it creates a sort of ambiguity.

3.    Ambiguous Figure-Ground Relationship

This type of relationship makes it quite difficult for spectators to separate ground from the figure in design. In such designs, viewers cannot detect a focal point. Although, it is ideal for creating patterns in the logo.

HOW TO USE THE CONCEPT IN DESIGNING

So, if you want to create distinct figure ground relationships in the logo, here is what you can do:

  • Create stark figure-ground distinction by using contrasting colors. Using warm colors against a cool and cool against a warm background you can create a compelling contrast. This is especially helpful for users to recognize and read the text.
  • Make the ground non-existent in your logo, so that figure in design dominates the ground. You need to amplify and prominent the figure elements so that the ground becomes insignificant.
  • A blurred background acts well against a text. It is another way to make the figure-ground relationship blatant. In fact, it is a key technique in filmmaking.

END OF THE LINE

So, these are the things you should consider about making your business logo according to the figure-ground relationship concept.

]]> Logo is the prime branding element for any company.
Your logo identifies your product. It is also considered as the core idea of the brand.
While designing logo, it is important to decide the shape, font and colors of the logo.
Once you build strong image of your brand, you can change your logo slightly but it takes time Your logo should not be adaptable, it has to be unique.

To design your logo hire logo designer who knows fundamental of graphic designing and also consumer behavior. An experience designer can help you in a professional way and consult you if your idea conflict with the audience’ perception.

Logo Adjustable in all Color Palette

Logo is the first impression of any brand. People look at the packaging and branding of product before buying it.
Use bright, bold and loud colors to take hold of consumers’ attention. Do not use flat colors, it can be adaptable easily. Choses color transitions of same color family to differentiate from other brands.
Using more than 2 colors distract viewers and their brand recall towards your brand. Use less but attractive colors to make your consumers remember it whenever they see your brand and they can relate your teaser campaigns to your brand easily. Before giving final look to your logo, put it on different color palettes to see if the logo is decipherable in all background colors. 

Broken Typography

Typography has a vast area to play with the letters. Break your letters in a creative way to give meaning to your brand. Make your viewer look twice and think about your brand later.
You can break A’s or straight letters to make and arrow and show the growth of your brand in this way.

Expressive textured letters

Use of serif and san serifs is important following any one form, illustrate letters according to the nature of your brand. If your brand nature is sweet and delicate, you can make it like that by soften the corners of letters. If you are a scuba driver, your logo could be water textured.
You can give horror look to the letters if your brand relate to some dangerous element.

Symmetrical letters

Patterns and repetition eliminates the chance of replication. Simple sleek letters are so mainstream, use geometrical letters to change the look and feel of your logo. Patterns create involvement of the viewers as they try to find the meaning behind it and are pleasant to eyes.
It also bring the homogeneity and empathy in all of your branding element.

Doodle your logo

Best way to create emotional attachment and uniqueness in your logo is to sketch it on the paper first.
Take a paper and draw whatever comes in your mind. Hand drawn logos are always unique and exclusive.

GIFs logo for web

2018 will be the era of moving pictures. Play puzzle with your logo. Make GIFS of your logo. Replace and rearrange tagline, object or text in the GIF.
GIFs and animation always attract customers and it brings a bit of entertainment for them.

Bring revolution in logo design by following the above creative ideas. Your logo deSignagency should know how to manipulate your logo according to the latest trend.
