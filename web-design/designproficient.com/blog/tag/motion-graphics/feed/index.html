]]> Making money as a Graphic Designer is not everyone’s cup of tea. Graphic designing may seem an easy and effortless profession but the truth isn’t that smooth. It requires talent and perfect promotion both to earn a living as a graphic designer. Graphic designing is strictly a creative and artistic skill and people who are lost in art, usually are not that proactive with selling their art to the right people in the right way. Most artists eventually get underestimated due to lack of promotion and PR. If you are one of those graphic artists who is looking for ways to extend their income through correct routes then this blog might save you some efforts to capitalize your skill.

ALSO READ: How much to charge clients as a new designer?

Pursue a Graphic Designing Degree

Graphic Designing is more of a skill and mostly people earn this skill through various courses and proper training to get their hands on tools. There are many institutions around the world which offer legitimate degree programs on graphic designing. We recommend you to earn a degree if you are skilled enough with designing to make your profile more professional. This makes it easier for you to get hired by good companies.

Build an impressive Portfolio

Create an account on any online portfolio websites such as Behance and add your best pieces of work there. An impressive portfolio plays a convincing role to get more clients online and this is going to be more impactful than your resume or CV. So if you don’t yet have a maintained portfolio, now is the time to start with it.

Be a Freelancer

Freelancing is the most practiced mode of money making by graphic designers. Freelance websites like Upwork and Fiverr has given a privilege of earning in millions to many individuals through high convenience and guaranteed clients. All you would need to take care of will be keep yourself away from scammers who don’t pay after getting projects done.

Sell your files online

Create your best pieces of digital artwork and you can sell them on multiple platforms online. If you are okay with selling away your copyrights then you can be one of those millionaires who mint money through online work!

Earning money is not an easy task until you find the hack of getting into the right direction. Discovering your skills is important but selling them is also very important!

]]> Animated motion graphic videos are a popular choice for marketers and brand owners. They are mainly used to describe functionality and uses of any product or service. Companies prefer to place these videos on YouTube and landing pages of the website for promotion purpose.

Animated motion graphics video production requires a fine budget and set of skills that are competitive enough to handle all the technical aspects. A complete team works together to generate a great video but still, sometimes the response of video is not appropriate. Maybe because producers and brand owners neglect some aspects that are essential for the creation of a great piece of art. In this article you will read about five tips regarding animated motion graphic videos.

ALSO READ: Interesting Facts about 2D and 3D animation

Script

It is commonly assumed that for an animated motion graphics video only technical skills are enough. But in actual creativity, concept and a script all have their own value. Script directly supports the animation and in case there are some loopholes in your animation, only a good script can cover them well.  Script provoke the audience to view the video till its end.

Technology

Advancement of technology is essential to give a new life to animation. Speed, colors, lights and overall look all suffer a lot when production team uses outdated technology. For 3D animation the more you invest in technology the more productive results you receive.

Tools

There are a number of platforms where you can create animated motion graphics. Each of the programs allows users to avail certain tools. According to your niche, you can also install add-ons for ease and quality of work. Some tools are free whereas for many you have to pay some bucks. Smart use of tools can help production team to create attractive animations in less time.

Realistic

Try to make every element, character, and background look realistic. The more real it looks the more audience get attracted to it. No matter you are creating a cartoon or fictional character or there are only products in the video, still, the things should look close to real. For example along with characters also focus on shadows.

Application

PowToon, Sparkol, Wideo, Moovly, WhatFix are some of the applications that can help production team to create amazing animated motion graphics videos. Many applications provide free templates so that you can start animation process in an organized way.

Hopefully, you like the motioned information and find it helpful for your upcoming motion graphic video projects.
