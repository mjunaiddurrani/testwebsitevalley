Which social platform has the highest following in 2020!

Facebook is the first-ever and the biggest social media platform with many users that engaged them and enabled them to talk to each other. YouTube is the social media platform that consists of video content in it. It is a bit different from other platforms. You can only share your video content and reviews here. It was launched in the year 2005. Its current headquarters are in San Bruno, California.

Millions of videos are present over YouTube, and people can upload videos of songs, life hacks, informative videos, and much more. Millions of small and large businesses are running over this app, and people promote their services and business through videos.

We all are aware of WhatsApp messenger. If we will say that WhatsApp is the most significant source for text and voice messages, then it wouldn’t be wrong. Facebook messenger or we just simply called it Messenger, basically it is also a chatting platform. It was introduced by Facebook for chatting over text, later updates were made to it, and now it functions almost the same as WhatsApp messenger.

It was first developed in 2008, and later it updated its services in 2010. And since then, it has grown its user to 1.3 billion.

 

  • WeChat 

WeChat comprises of same features as WhatsApp and Messenger, but what is unique in it that you besides chat and calls you can do some online shopping and payments thorough WeChat.

It was first introduced in the year 2010, and till now, WeChat has approx.1.1 Billion users.

Instagram is a popular social media platform; most of us are aware of it, have an Instagram app on our phones, and use it regularly. Final Thoughts 

We all know social media platforms are the most significant source to represent our brand, to make it unique among the competitors.
