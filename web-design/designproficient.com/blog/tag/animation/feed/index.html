]]> The history of animation is linked to ancient Egyptian era, people of that times used to carve sculptures that repeat their motions in a specific pattern. The technique at that time used to be considered as some spiritual power is making the idol to move. Spinning wheels were also used to create the ambiance of motion of objects creating some certain moves.

The idea, later on, was adopted by illustrator and sketchers to create a series of pictures. These pictures were created and places in a way that when compiled and played speedily, they seem to create an animation.

2D Animation

Though technology is spreading its wings day by day and in the market even 6D animation videos are being created by several companies still the demand for 2D animation is high. Many companies prefer to place a 2D animation video on landing pages of their website along with social media pages to provide an explanation about the features, uses and working of their products. The reason is that 2D animation production consumes less time and is cost-effective.

Another reason to prefer 2D animation video is that their production doesn`t require exceptional or very advanced technology. The software’s of 2D animation can efficiently function on even windows 7. So it`s quite simple to produce 2D animation, and yet it benefits marketing brand a lot. For products and services that are common, simple and a necessity, 2D animation videos are ideal.

 

3D Animation

For luxury products, fashion or design related products and services and digital marketing firms’ 3D animation videos are a potent tool to generate a brand`s impact on the customers. It helps to convert potential clients into regular buyers. 3D animation videos are more realistic than 2D animation videos, and their influence is thrice attractive as well. Apparently, to generate a 3D animation video, you need a reasonable budget. The production of 3D animation is expensive and consumes more time.

The complex and multi-functional products are best explained through 3D animation because all the sides and aspects of products are defined well, and viewers can experience a more realistic perspective of the products. To stylize the image of any product or service and present your brand in a competitive way 3D animation video is the best idea.

 

The facts that I have shared are best for marketing and branding activities linked to animation videos. Hopefully, you enjoyed reading this piece of information about 2D and 3D animation videos.

]]> In today’s digitally competitive world, the task of brand websites not only rests with selling products but also to entertain the users and to make their experience of using the website unique and enjoyable. In order to tap the delight of the users, web designers have to design the animation in an interactive and amusing manner.

Animation has become a core element of Web design, posing a challenge for the web developers and app makers to stand out with more interactive and creative ideas. Your animated brand design should build a strong connection turning the first comer into a permanent user.

Engaging animations directs the attention of the user to the product creating lasting impressions. A successful animated design is the one that reflects the theme of the product and builds an emotional connection with the consumer. You can also develop such animation by following the 5 very important techniques.

Fun-filled Interactive Experience

While you develop a website or an app, make sure to design a pleasurable and fun-filled medium of interaction with the user. Turn their experience into a moment of everlasting joy; your animation should give them the pleasure of enjoyment.

Entertain Them Whilst Loading

Website designers usually fail to hook the users while long hours of loading. It is the most crucial and unavoidable time when you need to keep the user involved and entertained. Make the wait lesser boring for them by incorporating creative animations. Surprise them; bring up something amazing and unexpected to create a distraction.

Also read: Supercharging Video Content with 3D Animated Explainer Videos

Efficient Error Handling

Technical errors can occur anytime, and their absolute avoidance is almost impossible in the digital world. You can’t stop them from happening but you can handle them in a way that causes the least discomfort to the user. If the app fails, the backup should be well-crafted with immediate feedback acknowledging the consumer’s query and providing an immediate solution.

Comfort Users In Difficult Tasks

If the work has many intricacies and involves tension, try to present it in a simpler and easier way by using funny and friendly animations. Help their stress reduce while the task by distracting them with either animated cartoons or any other digital content.

Cast Strong First Impressions

The first appearance of your digital product will set most of the impact on the user either positive or negative. Designing attractive animations will build a more impressive outlook and personality of the product. Use your first impression in holding consumers’ attention and delivering your message in the desired way.

These simple ways can help the branding companies and website development agencies in making the most of the animations for improving the user’s experience.  Explore ideas and be creative with your designs, use techniques like collapsing menus, highlighting content, scrolling, and hovering tabs. The more you will amaze, the stronger you will connect.
